Bibingka

Bibingka is a classic Filipino delicacy that can be easily made using eggs, cheese, and grated coconut, it is especially popular during the Christmas season. Sold outside of churches during the nine-day Misa de Gallo. -retrieved from www.kawalingpinoy.com

Ingredients and recipe gathered from kawalingpinoy.com and knorr.com

Ingredients Quantity
Rice flour 1 cup
Salt 1/8 teaspoon
Baking powder 2 1/2 teaspoon
Butter 3 tablespoons
Granulated Sugar 1/2 kilogram
Coconut Milk 1 cup
Fresh Milk 1/4 cup
Grated Cheese 1/2 cup
Pre-cut banana leaf 1 piece
Grated Coconut 1/4 cut
Raw eggs 3 pieces
Optional Margarine 1 tablespoon

Step 1

Let's begin by sifting and combining rice flour and baking powder together. Set aside. Whisk sugar and butter until well mixed.

Step 2

Bring Knorr Complete Recipe Mix Ginataang Gulay dissolved in water to a boil then bring down to a simmer until thick. Let this cool.

Step 3

In a bowl, beat egg until light before adding the milk and cooled gata. Combine all dry and liquid ingredients together

Step 4

Pour mixture in a greased round baking pan lined with banana leaf then cook in between coals or in an oven. When bibingka is halfway set, top it with salted eggs and cheese.

Step 5

Cook until inserted toothpick at the center comes out clean.

Step 6

Serve topped with margarine, sugar, and niyog. What can be more fun than enjoying a warm and nice serving of Bibingka with family and friends.
